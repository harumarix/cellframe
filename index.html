02

Framework

Framework advantages

Scalability

Our framework is based on original sharding implementation: high-performance Cells — [DAG +Blockchain] elementary units with up to 100,000 TPS.

Utilization

Cellframe is a low-level architecture solution build
on plain C. It efficiently interacts with hardware, and it can be launched on a wide range of devices: your smartphone, laptop, enterprise server system, or even smart fridge.

Security

Cellframe is protected by the implementation of quantum and post-quantum cryptographic signatures by default with encryption variability feature. We have 0% of third party code.

03

Communication

Interchain communication

Read more about architecture of Cellframe interchain communications in our blog.

Future of dApps and blockchain-based economy rely on fast and secure transfer of value and information across public and private blockchains.

Interoperability is a hot topic in the industry,
widely researched and developed by a number
of experienced teams. Nevertheless, at the moment, there is no ready-to-use solution for blockchain interoperability.

Cellframe is developing a solution for inter-blockchain communications based on Cells approach
and pzp communications model.

03

Quantum

Quantum threat is real

Quantum computer with logical qubits is coming in the next 10-15 years.
The NSA (US National Security Agency) and NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology)
are preparing for it and developing standards for post-quantum cryptography since 2015.

Read more about quantum computing and its threat to blockchain in our blog.

PQ Encryption by default

We had successfully implemented several quantum safe signatures including finalists
of second round of NIST PQC contest, and we will implement several more by 2021.

Encryption Variability

Encryption Variability. Truly quantum resistant blockchain is build with encryption variability feature.

Quantum Keys

Quantum Keys. Implementation of quantum key exchange by Q3 2020.
